## Usage Notes

- **Personalize the hook** -- reference something specific (their Google rating, a recent project, their website, their industry)
- **Keep it under 150 words** -- cold emails should be scannable
- **One CTA only** -- always the Calendly link
- **Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9am-11am** in their timezone
- **Follow up 2-3 times** before moving on (3 days, then 7 days apart)
